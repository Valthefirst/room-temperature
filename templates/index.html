By Da Zhuo Xie and Valentine Nneji

About the Project

This project was made using a raspberry pi with the purpose to find the relationship between the presence of someone in the room and the temperature of the room. Our hypothesis is that the room gets warmer with the presence of people inside the room. We use ultrasonic sensors to detect the presence of people entering or exiting the room. The temperature sensor records the temperature of the room every 15 minutes.
