About Iot 2 Final Project

By Da Zhuo Xie and Valentine Nneji



About the Project

This project was made using a Raspberry Pi with the purpose of finding the relationship between the presence of someone in the room and the temperature of the room. Our hypothesis is that the room gets warmer with the presence of people inside. Ultrasonic sensors detect the presence of people entering or exiting the room, while the temperature sensor records the room temperature every 15 minutes.



Technologies Used

For the hardware, we used a Raspberry Pi, ultrasonic sensors, and a temperature sensor to track movement and temperatures. We also used wires and other connection components. As for the software, we utilized Flask, JavaScript, ChartJS, HTML, and CSS.
